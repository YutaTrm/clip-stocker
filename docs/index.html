ClipStocker

Save and organize your favorite short videos in one place

About

ClipStocker is a bookmark manager for short-form videos. Save videos from your favorite platforms and organize them like a photo album on your iOS device.

Supported Platforms

YouTube Shorts TikTok Instagram Reels X (Twitter)

Features

  • Save videos directly from other apps using the Share Extension
  • Automatic thumbnail and title fetching
  • Organize videos with custom tags
  • Grid view layout similar to iOS Photos
  • Quick access to open videos in their original apps
  • All data stored locally on your device

Requirements

  • iOS 17.0 or later
  • iPhone
