Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Overview

ClipStocker ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS application.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

ClipStocker is designed with privacy in mind. All your data stays on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

Data Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Video URLs you bookmark
  • Thumbnails and titles (fetched from public APIs)
  • Custom tags you create
  • Custom images you set for bookmarks

This data is stored using Apple's SwiftData framework and remains on your device. It is not synced to iCloud or any cloud service.

Third-Party Services

The app makes requests to the following services to fetch video metadata:

  • YouTube - To fetch video thumbnails and titles via oEmbed API
  • TikTok - To fetch video thumbnails and titles via oEmbed API

These requests only include the video URL and do not include any personal information or device identifiers.

Analytics

ClipStocker does not use any analytics or tracking services. We do not collect usage data, crash reports, or any other telemetry.

Advertising

ClipStocker does not display any advertisements.

Children's Privacy

ClipStocker does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app does not require any personal information to function.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data by:

  • Deleting individual bookmarks within the app
  • Uninstalling the app (this removes all associated data)

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
